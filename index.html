About

DAWoC is a month long programme exculsively for students of DA-IICT where students contribute to a variety of Open-Source Projects under the guidance of experienced Mentors. The program encourages students to get engaged with open source and also additionally prepares them for a lot of open source programs, like Google Summer of Code aka GSoC and many more.

Open Source For The Win!

Why Participate?

adb group card_giftcard

Whether you are a mentor or a student, participating in DAWoC only proves beneficial to you. As a student you will be able to learn alot of new open-source technologies and teach you how to work on software development projects in a bigger team. It will also provide the students a gateway to the enormous open source community. As for the Mentors they will be able to get alot of new additions to their projects. And Finally there will be them T-Shirts and goodies which everyone looks forward to!
